Paneer Spring Roll

Paneer spring rolls are a popular Indo-Chinese fusion appetizer that combine the crispy texture of a classic spring roll with the rich, savory flavors of Indian cottage cheese.

Noodles

Noodles are a type of food typically made from unleavened dough which is rolled flat and cut, stretched, or extruded into long strips or strings. Noodles are a staple food in many cultures and made into a variety of shapes.
